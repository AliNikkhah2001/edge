# Temporal Synchronization

- **Hardware trigger**: shared pulse → deterministic alignment  
- **Master–slave**: master camera drives exposure of others  
- **NTP**: clock alignment with small jitter  
- **Content-based**: SynNet (pose-sequence matching) to infer offsets
